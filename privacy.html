Privacy Policy for BunBurger Shop

At BunBurger, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our customers. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to ensure its protection.

Information We Collect:

1. Personal Information:
We may collect personal information, such as your name, email address, phone number, and address when you place an order or sign up for our newsletter.

2. Payment Information:
When making a purchase, we collect payment information, including credit card details. Please note that we do not store sensitive payment information after the transaction is complete.

3. Log Data:
We automatically collect certain information when you visit our website, including your IP address, browser type, and device information.

How We Use Your Information:

1. Order Processing:
We use your personal information to process and fulfill your orders, including order confirmation, shipment, and customer support.

2. Communications:
With your consent, we may send you promotional emails about new products, special offers, or other updates. You can opt-out of these communications at any time.

3. Improving our Services:
We analyze user data to enhance and personalize your experience on our website, improve our products, and optimize our services.

Information Sharing:

1. Third-Party Service Providers:
We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in delivering our products and services.

2. Legal Compliance:
We may disclose personal information when required by law or in response to legal requests.

Security:

1. Data Security:
We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Your Choices:

1. Opt-Out:
You can opt-out of receiving promotional emails by following the instructions in the email or contacting us directly.

2. Access and Update:
You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Please contact us for assistance.

Changes to This Privacy Policy:

We reserve the right to update our Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@bunburger.com.
